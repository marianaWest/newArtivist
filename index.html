FAST & UNRESTRICTED

The NEAR blockchain system makes the process of helping people and causes accessible to everyone, regardless of geographic location, language, or currency. Artivist promotes a solidarity economy through cryptocurrency, with no intermediaries, to help your cause without bureaucracy.

With Artivist, helping social and environmental causes is simple, safe, and transparent. With no mediators, all proceeds go directly to the NGO and cause of your choice and the artist receives for their work automatically.
